What Makes a Film Successful?

What Makes a Film Successful?

4 Main Ingredients:

Budget

While not always the case, the more money you have to throw into your project, the better your returns will be. However, some of the most successful movies had very limited budgets but still resulted in sizable revenue.

Genre

The genre of your movie determines who will watch your fill. Some genres are universally loved, while some only attract a certain type of crowd.

Popularity

Our data generates a popularity index based on the buzz that is created within social media about your project.

Vote Count

How many people vote for your movie is a strong indication of popularity and thus, return on investment. Largely bad reviews and votes will hurt your potential.
